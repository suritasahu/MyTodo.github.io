About MyTodo

MyTodo is a simple yet powerful task management tool that helps you stay organized and on top of your daily tasks. Whether it's for personal use or work-related tasks, MyTodo makes it easy to keep track of what needs to be done.

Developed by Suritasahu, MyTodo was built with simplicity and user-friendliness in mind. Our goal is to help users streamline their task management and enhance productivity.

For any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to us!

How to Use the App

  1. Add a New Task: On the homepage, fill in the "Todo Title" and "Todo Description" fields, then click the "Add" button. Your new task will appear in the list below.
  2. View Your Tasks: All your tasks are listed under the "Your Todo's" section. You can see the task title, description, and the date it was created.
  3. Edit a Task: To modify a task, click the "Update" button next to the task you want to change. You'll be redirected to a page where you can edit the title and description. After editing, click "Update" to save your changes.
  4. Delete a Task: If you want to remove a task, click the "Delete" button next to it. The task will be permanently removed from the list.
  5. Search for a Task: You can use the search bar at the top to quickly find a specific task by title or keyword.

Start adding your tasks and stay productive!
